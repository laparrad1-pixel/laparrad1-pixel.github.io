AbstractA few years ago Facebook Artificial Intelligence Research Team (FAIR) had quite the scare. Researchers caught wind of its AI seemingly on its own accord. Create and use its own language that the scientists had no knowledge of. What made it frightening was that no one told the AI software to perform this action, but more terrifying than this was they did not know what it was saying. They just know it was using some new code that they were unaware of. Thankfully, they decided the best course of action was to kill the program before it was able to do any real damage.
